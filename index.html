About Pat

Property Advisor.
Project Strategist.
REALTOR®.

Pat Miazga sees what most agents miss. After 22 years in BC real estate — and a background in commercial construction, project management, and hands-on renovation work — Pat evaluates every property on a level most agents simply can't. Construction quality, hidden costs, renovation potential, long-term value. His clients don't just get a transaction. They get an honest assessment from someone who has seen everything and will tell them the truth.

From foreclosures to development land, multi-family buildings to commercial leases, strata dissolutions to estate sales — Pat has handled it all across Greater Vancouver and beyond. He is a RE/MAX Hall of Fame recipient, MLS® Medallion Club member, and has maintained his license in good standing since January 2004.

Three years ago, Pat relocated his family to the South Okanagan. He knows what the move feels like, what questions come up, and what surprises people — because he lived it. He still maintains his Coquitlam office, so he can list your Lower Mainland home and find your Okanagan property without ever handing you off.

When Pat isn't working on a deal, he's usually building something — saunas, greenhouses, hot sauces that friends keep asking for. The same instinct that drives his real estate practice: with the right knowledge and a little work, most things can be made significantly better.

He speaks English and Polish. He holds active security clearances. And he picks up the phone.

Life in the South Okanagan

What you're actually moving to.

Climate

More sunny days than almost anywhere in Canada. Hot summers, mild winters — a dramatic change from Lower Mainland grey.

Wine & Food

Oliver and Osoyoos sit at the heart of BC wine country. World-class wineries, fruit stands, orchards, u-pick farms, and farmers markets at your doorstep.

Recreation

Quading, skiing, golf, boating, fishing, hiking, RVing — an outdoor playground with something for every season.

Family & Community

Smaller schools, safer streets, kids playing outside. Pat raises his own family here and speaks honestly about what community life looks like day to day.

Property Opportunity

Acreages, orchards, hobby farms, lakefront — property types that simply don't exist in Metro Vancouver, at a fraction of the price.

Honest Picture

Pat will tell you what services are available locally, what you'll drive for, and what rural ownership actually means day to day. No sugar coating.
